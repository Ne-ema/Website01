ABOUT US

Our Story

Silver Sands School was founded in the year 1998 by Jenny Silver a white settler living in Kenya, and began as a small preparatory school in her back kitchen.At first the students in the school were all white but gradually Jenny welcomed black students in the school. In 2000 she managed to acquire a relatively huge piece of land and build classes and in she they managed to have her first candidate class which was a huge milestone.In 2004 Silver Sands was made a boarding School with a space of over 1000 student occcupants.

Why choose Silver Sands

Silver Sands School equips students with skills that enable them to be able to contribute positively to society.
