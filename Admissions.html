About Admissions

The academic year begins in late August 2021 and it is preferable to bring your child at that time of the year.However a student can be accepted at any time in the year.Acceptance to the school entails an evaluation of the child's previous academic record and an interview if this is practical to arrange.An entry test is also conducted to help in the placement process.


Admissions policy

All admissions to Silver Sands is done by a senior member of the staff.When seeking admission prospective students are required to provide academic transcripts and reports.A student goes through the admissions process if they are determined to contribute positively to the school and go at par with the school's curriculum.


Admissions Process

A student who is interested in joining Silver Sands should complete the application form online and forward it to the school.Copies of examination reports and former examination results should also be forwarded to the school.
